Adventures in Fire Island

My recent trip to Fire Island was an incredible experience exploring this beautiful barrier island off Long Island. The pristine beaches, rolling sand dunes, and diverse wildlife made for unforgettable memories. From hiking through maritime forests to watching stunning sunsets over the Atlantic Ocean, Fire Island offers the perfect escape into nature.
